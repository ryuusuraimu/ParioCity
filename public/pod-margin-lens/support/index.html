Basic troubleshooting

Extension does not open

Open Chrome's Extensions page, confirm POD Margin Lens is enabled, then try reopening the popup. Restart Chrome if needed.

Products disappeared

Product data is stored in the local Chrome profile. Switching profiles, removing the extension, or clearing browser data may remove saved products.

Calculations look wrong

Check selling price, product cost, shipping cost, target CPA, discount rate, fee rate, and fixed fee. Results are estimates based on your inputs.

How to remove the extension

Open Chrome's Extensions page, find POD Margin Lens, and choose Remove.
