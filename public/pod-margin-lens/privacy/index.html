POD Margin Lens Privacy Policy

POD Margin Lens is designed as a local-only Chrome Extension.

  • POD Margin Lens stores user-entered product calculation data locally in the user's browser using chrome.storage.local.
  • The extension does not collect personal data.
  • The extension does not transmit data to external servers.
  • The extension does not connect to Shopify, Printify, Meta Ads, or any external API.
  • The extension does not track browsing activity.
  • The extension does not use analytics.
  • The extension does not sell, share, or use user data for advertising.
  • All calculations are performed locally on the user's device.
