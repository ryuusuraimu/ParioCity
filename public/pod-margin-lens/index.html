What it does

  • Add POD products manually.
  • Estimate break-even CPA and post-ad profit.
  • Rank products as Strong, Testable, Risky, or Loss.
  • Store product calculation data locally in Chrome.

Why ad-cost safety matters

POD sellers often compare many products before running ads. POD Margin Lens helps estimate which products have more room for target CPA before launching tests.

Key calculations

Break-even CPA

The estimated maximum ad cost per purchase before the product becomes unprofitable.

Post-ad profit

The estimated product profit after subtracting the target CPA.

Ad safety ratio

Break-even CPA divided by target CPA, used to support ranking decisions.

Rank

Strong, Testable, Risky, or Loss based on the ad safety ratio.

Formula reference

Revenue = Selling Price × (1 - Discount Rate)
Payment Fee = Revenue × Fee Rate + Fixed Fee
Pre-Ad Profit = Revenue - Product Cost - Shipping Cost - Payment Fee
Break-even CPA = Pre-Ad Profit
Post-Ad Profit = Pre-Ad Profit - Target CPA
Ad Safety Ratio = Break-even CPA / Target CPA
